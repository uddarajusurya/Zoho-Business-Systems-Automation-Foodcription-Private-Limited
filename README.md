# Zoho Business Systems & Automation – Foodcription Private Limited

## 🚀 Overview
Designed and implemented end-to-end business systems using the Zoho ecosystem to automate food delivery operations, streamline financial processes, and enable data-driven decision-making at Foodcription Private Limited.

---

## 🧠 Business Challenges
- Manual order and delivery tracking leading to inefficiencies  
- Lack of integration between operations, inventory, and finance systems  
- High chances of errors in order reconciliation and inventory tracking  
- Time-consuming manual reporting processes  
- Limited visibility into real-time business performance  

---

## 💡 Solutions Implemented

### 🔹 Operations & Delivery Management Application
- Developed a custom application using Zoho Creator for managing orders and deliveries  
- Automated task assignments for operations and delivery teams  
- Improved tracking and coordination of daily deliveries  

---

### 🔹 Inventory & Material Handling System
- Built a system to track raw material usage integrated with Zoho Inventory  
- Ensured accurate inventory tracking and reduced manual errors  

---

### 🔹 Finance & Order Reconciliation System
- Integrated Zoho Creator with Zoho Books for invoicing and payment tracking  
- Automated reconciliation processes to improve financial accuracy  

---

### 🔹 Analytics & Reporting Dashboards
- Developed dashboards using Zoho Analytics to track:
  - Sales performance  
  - Delivery metrics  
  - Operational KPIs  
- Reduced manual reporting efforts by **50%**  

---
